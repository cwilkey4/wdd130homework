Chopper Meals

Between work, homework, and tuition, mealtime is usually downgraded to whatever is fast and cheap. Convenient? Yes. Unhealthy? Maybe a bit. Boring and repetitive? Absolutely yes.

Here, our hope is to encourage students through an online medium to get a little more creative and nutritious with what they eat while working with these limitations.

Here you'll find meal plans with simple recipes, a basic dietary education, and a means of getting ingredients and supplies onto your shelves!
