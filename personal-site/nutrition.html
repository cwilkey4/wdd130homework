Nutrition... Why?

Yeah, this is the boring stuff. Plenty of people get along well enough, especially in their young adult years. However, this time also happens to be a critical to future health. A lot of health problems are tied directly to a person's diet, and even more of those take tons of time to develop. By the time symptoms start to manifest, it's generally difficult to reverse, if possible in the first place. Why not make a few changes now?
